About this instrument

This tool operationalizes the regenerative business continuum proposed by Reed (2007) and adapted for business strategy by Hahn & Tampe (2021), grounding it in the eight sustainability principles of the Framework for Strategic Sustainable Development (Broman & Robèrt, 2017).

Two complementary views

The Starter uses 23 reframed Future-Fit Break-Even Goals as principle- aligned indicators. The Deep Dive matrix surfaces cross-principle trade-offs invisible to a single-axis view. Use both for a richer picture.

Methodological note

Scores are diagnostic, not absolute. The instrument supports practitioner conversation about a feature's direction of travel — degenerative, neutral, or regenerative — not a quantitative impact measurement.
